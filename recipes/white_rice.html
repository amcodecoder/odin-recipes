White Rice

Originated from Nigeria

The indeginious food of Hausa tribe in Africa

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomotoes
  • Paper
  • Onions
  • Seasons

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Make a soup with Tomotoes, paper, Onions and the seasons
  3. Take a plate of the cooked rice
  4. Add the cooked soup to it
  5. Enjoy
